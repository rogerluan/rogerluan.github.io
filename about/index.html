About

During my childhood, I always hated reading. But that changed later in life when I started reading a lot of technical articles and other interesting content. Then I realized I never really hated reading itself, just reading fiction books that we were forced to read in school. Who'd thought?

Over the course of years I realized I could help the tech community by writing about topics that were too hard to find information about. This website hosts those articles!

This website is currently a work in progress. There're many things still being built, such as better menus, proper tags, metadata, better syntax highlighting for programming posts, and just a better visual aspect.
