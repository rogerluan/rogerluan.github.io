About Roger Oba

I’m a Software Engineer passionate about mobile development, proficient in Swift, Objective-C, and Kotlin. I’m analytical, solution-oriented, and have high UX standards. I currently lead mobile engineering at Tellus, a real estate superapp software company based in Palo Alto, California. With well-known investors backing the company, it has made significant headway in reimagining Real Estate for the modern era. Tellus is an app for real estate loans where investing is gamified and made easy, friendly and understandable, for newcomers and experts alike.

Tellus is tackling a $2 trillion annual TAM that’s ripe for disruption. We believe everyone should be able to access the steady returns of real estate and have exposure to this great asset class.

Tellus aligns with my passion for the fintech industry as well as high UX standards.
